## ⚠️ COMMON ISSUES & SOLUTIONS

### "Property not showing"
**Cause:** Developer name doesn't match  
**Solution:** Check exact spelling with your developer

### "Prices look wrong"
**Cause:** Old cached data  
**Solution:** 
- Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
- Wait 5 minutes
- Refresh page

### "Images aren't loading"
**Cause:** Bad image link  
**Solution:** Ask developer to check image URLs

### "Website is slow"
**Cause:** Many high-res images  
**Solution:** Compress images before sending

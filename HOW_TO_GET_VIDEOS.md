### **Step 2: Find Videos You LOVE**

Click on any video you like. Look for:

- ✅ Good lighting (sunset, golden hour)
- ✅ 10+ seconds long
- ✅ HD or 4K quality

---

### **Step 3: Get The Direct Video URL**

On the Pexels video page:
1. Click the **green "Free Download"** button
2. Choose **"Original" or "HD"** quality
3. The download starts — but **WAIT!**
4. **Right-click on the download** in your browser
5. Click **"Copy link address"**
6. You now have a URL like:
   ```
   https://videos.pexels.com/video-files/12345678/12345678-uhd_2560_1440_25fps.mp4
   ```
